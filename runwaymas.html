Runway Markings and Signs
跑道标志和标识

Runway markings vary depending on the type of operations conducted at the airport. A basic VFR runway may only have centerline markings and runway numbers.

跑道标志由机场的运行种类决定，一个基础的目视飞行规则跑道可能只有中线标志和跑道号。

Runway designators
跑道方位号

Runway numbers and letters are determined from the approach direction. The runway number is the whole number nearest one‐tenth the magnetic azimuth of the centerline of the runway, measured clockwise from the magnetic north. Since aircraft are affected by the wind during takeoffs and landings, runways are laid out according to the local prevailing winds. Runway Certain airports have two or even three runways laid out in the same direction. These are referred to as parallel runways and are distinguished by a letter added to the runway number (e.g., runway 36L (left), 36C (center), and 36R (right)). [Figure 1-1]

跑道号和字母根据进近方向来确定，跑道号是从磁北与跑道中线顺时针量取的磁方位角的十分之一取整。 由于飞机在起降过程中极易受风的影响，因此跑道是根据当地盛行风来设置的。 有的机场有两条甚至三条相同方向的跑道，这些跑道 被称为平行跑道，并在跑道号中加入字母来相互区分。 （例如：跑道36L(左)，跑道36C（中），跑道36R（右））[图1-1]

Runway centerline marking
跑道中线标志

The runway centerline identifies the center of the runway and provides alignment guidance during takeoff and landings. The centerline consists of a white line of uniformly spaced stripes and gaps.

跑道中线沿跑道中心画设，为起飞和着陆提供跟随引导。中线由长度相等的线段和间隔构成。

Runway aiming point marking
跑道瞄准点标志

The aiming point marking serves as a visual aiming point for a landing aircraft. These two rectangular markings consist of a broad white stripe located on each side of the runway centerline and approximately 1,000 feet from the landing threshold, as shown in FIG 1-3.

瞄准点标志为着陆飞机提供目视瞄准，这个标志由设置在跑道中心线两侧的 两个白色矩形宽条带组成，距离着陆方向跑道入口大约1000英尺。如图1-3。

Runway touchdown zone markers
跑道接地带标志

The touchdown zone markings identify the touchdown zone for landing operations and are coded to provide distance information in 500 feet (150m) increments. These markings consist of groups of one, two, and three rectangular bars symmetrically arranged in pairs about the runway centerline, as shown in FIG 1-3. For runways having touchdown zone markings on both ends, those pairs of markings which extend to within 900 feet (270 m) of the midpoint between the thresholds are eliminated.

接地带标志标志着着陆区域，接地带之间间隔为500英尺（150米），可用以提供距离信息。 这些标志由一个，两个或三个为一组，相对于跑道中心线对称的矩形条构成。如图1-3。对于两端都有接地带标志的跑道， 这些成对的标志只能延伸到两个跑道入口的中心点900英尺（270m）以外。

Runway side stripe marking
跑道边线标志

Runway side stripes delineate the edges of the runway. They provide a visual contrast between runway and the abutting terrain or shoulders. Side stripes consist of continuous white stripes located on each side of the runway as shown in FIG 1-3.

跑道边线标志划定了跑道的边缘，它们用以区分开跑道和跑道外的地形或道肩。跑道边线由跑道两侧连续的白色直线构成。如图1-3。

Runway shoulder markings
跑道道肩标志

Runway shoulder stripes may be used to supplement runway side stripes to identify pavement areas contiguous to the runway sides that are not intended for use by aircraft. Runway shoulder stripes are yellow. (See FIG 1-2.)

跑道道肩条带用于辅助跑道边线来区分跑道铺装区域和跑道两边与铺装区域相连但不给飞机使用的部分。 跑道道肩条带是黄色的（如图1-2）

Runway threshold markings
跑道入口标志

Runway threshold markings come in two configurations. They either consist of eight longitudinal stripes of uniform dimensions disposed symmetrically about the runway centerline (as shown in FIG 1-1) or the number of stripes is related to the runway width as indicated in TBL 1-1. A threshold marking helps identify the beginning of the runway that is available for landing. In some instances, the landing threshold may be relocated or displaced.

Relocated Runway Threshold
跑道入口调整

It is sometimes necessary, due to construction or runway maintenance, to close only a portion of a runway. When a portion of a runway is closed, the runway threshold is relocated as necessary. It is referred to as a relocated threshold and methods for identifying the relocated threshold vary. A common way for the relocated threshold to be marked is a ten foot wide white bar across the width of the runway and yellow arrow heads are placed across the width of the runway just prior to the threshold bar. [Figure 1-2]

有时由于工程建设或者跑道维护而强制关闭一部分跑道，当跑道的一部分关闭时，跑道入口便会 按需调整位置。这种情况被称作跑道入口调整，定义了跑道入口位置变化的一种情况。 通常用10英尺宽，横穿跑道的的白线，和在跑道入口白线外横向排列的黄色箭头作为跑道入口调整标志。[图1-4]

图1-4

When the threshold is relocated, the closed portion of the runway is not available for use by aircraft for takeoff or landing, but it is available for taxi. When a threshold is relocated, it closes not only a set portion of the approach end of a runway, but also shortens the length of the opposite direction runway.

当跑道入口被调整后，被关闭的那一部分跑道不可用作飞机起飞和着陆，只可以用作滑行， 入口调整不只是关闭了进近一侧的一部分跑道，它同时也缩短了另一侧进近方向的跑道长度。

Displaced Threshold
入口内移

A displaced threshold is a threshold located at a point on the runway other than the designated beginning of the runway. Displacement of a threshold reduces the length of runway available for landings. The portion of runway behind a displaced threshold is available for takeoffs in either direction, or landings from the opposite direction. A ten feet wide white threshold bar is located across the width of the runway at the displaced threshold, and white arrows are located along the centerline in the area between the beginning of the runway and displaced threshold. White arrow heads are located across the width of the runway just prior to the threshold bar. [Figure 1-5]

入口内移是指入口设置的位置不在跑道设计的末端。入口内移缩短了跑道着陆可用距离， 在入口内移之外的那一部分跑道可以在跑道的两个方向用于起飞，也可以在内移所在方向的反方向的跑道上用于着陆。 通常用10英尺宽，横穿跑道的的白线，和在跑道头和跑道入口之间沿跑道中线画设的白色箭头表示，并且在 入口白线外画设横向排列的白色箭头。[图 1-5]

Blast pad / stop ways
防吹坪 / 停止道

Blast pad / stop ways markings are used to show pavement areas aligned with the runway that are unusable for landing, takeoff, and taxiing. They are yellow chevrons.（FIG 1-4）

防吹坪/停止道标志用于标出跑道延伸出来不能用于起飞，着陆和滑行的道面，它们是黄色的V形。（图1-6）

Demarcation Bar
分界线

A demarcation bar delineates a runway with a displaced threshold from a blast pad / stopway, or taxiway that precedes the runway. A demarcation bar is 3 feet (1m) wide and yellow, since it is not located on the runway, as shown in FIG 1-7.

分界线用于分隔调整过的跑道入口和防吹坪/停止道、滑行道。分界线宽3英尺（1米），因为它不在跑道上，所以是黄色的，如图1-7。
