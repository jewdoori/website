Lift
升力

Low Pressure Above
上表面低压

The difference in curvature of the upper and lower surfaces of the wing builds up the lift force. Air flowing over the top surface of the wing must reach the trailing edge of the wing in the same amount of time as the air flowing under the wing. To do this, the air passing over the top surface moves at a greater velocity than the air passing below the wing because of the greater distance it must travel along the top surface. This increased velocity, according to Bernoulli’s Principle, means a corresponding decrease in pressure on the surface. [Figure 1-1] This lowered pressure is a component of total lift. The pressure difference between the upper and lower surface of a wing alone does not account for the total lift force produced.

机翼上下表面的弯度差异产生了升力。流过上表面的空气必须与流过下表面的空气 在同一时间到达机翼后缘，为了达到这一点，由于上表面空气的运动距离更长，上表面的空气流速 大于下表面。根据伯努利定律，速度增大使对应的压力减小。 [图1-1] 这种低压是总升力的组成部分之一，上下表面的压力差不是机翼总升力的唯一成因。

Downwash
下洗流

Because air pressure on the top of the wing is lower than the pressure below the wing. Near the tips of the wing, the air is free to move from the region of high pressure into the region of low pressure. Vortices are formed at the wing tips. [Figure 1-2]

因为机翼上表面的空气压力比下表的压力低。在翼尖区域，空气可从高压区域流向低压区域。涡流在翼尖处产生。[图1-2]

The wing tip vortices produce a downwash of air behind the wing which is very strong near the wing tips and decreases toward the wing root. [Figure 1-3] Applying Newton’s third law, the reaction of this downward backward flow results in an upward forward force on the airfoil.

翼尖涡流在机翼后方产生在翼尖到翼根逐渐减弱的下洗流。[图1-3] 根据牛顿第三定律，向后下方的这种气流对翼型产生向前向上的反作用力。

High Pressure Below
下表面高压

A certain amount of lift is generated by pressure conditions underneath the airfoil. Because of the manner in which air flows underneath the airfoil, a positive pressure results, particularly at higher angles of attack. At a point close to the leading edge, the airflow is virtually stopped (stagnation point) and then gradually increases speed. [Figure 1-4] At some point near the trailing edge, it again reaches a velocity equal to that on the upper surface. In conformance with Bernoulli’s principle, where the airflow was slowed beneath the airfoil, a positive upward pressure was created. Since the pressure differential between the upper and lower surface of the airfoil increases, total lift increases. Both Bernoulli’s Principle and Newton’s Laws are in operation whenever lift is being generated by an airfoil.

翼型的底部产生一定大小的升力，由于空气流过翼型底部会产生正压力， 尤其是在大迎角情况下。在靠近前缘德一个点上，气流停止运动（驻点）随后逐渐增大速度[图1-4]， 在靠近后缘的一点，它的速度再次与上表面的速度相等。 根据伯努利定律，当翼型下表面速度减小时，产生向上的正压力。 由于翼型上下表面的压力差增大，总升力增大。当翼型产生升力时，无论何时， 伯努利定律和牛顿定律都在同时发挥作用。

Angle of Incidence
安装角

The acute angle the wing chord makes with the longitudinal axis of the aircraft is called the angle of incidence, or the angle of wing setting. [Figure 1-5] The angle of incidence in most cases is a fixed, built-in angle. When the leading edge of the wing is higher than the trailing edge, the angle of incidence is said to be positive. The angle of incidence is negative when the leading edge is lower than the trailing edge of the wing.

机翼翼弦与飞机纵轴形成的锐角称作安装角，或叫做机翼安装角。[图1-5] 在多数情况下安装角是固定不变的角度。当机翼前缘的位置高于机翼后缘， 安装角为正，机翼前缘低于后缘，安装角为负。

图1-5

Angle of Attack
迎角（攻角）

The chord of an airfoil or wing section is an imaginary straight line that passes through the section from the leading edge to the trailing edge, as shown in Figure 1-6. The chord line provides one side of an angle that ultimately forms the AOA. The other side of the angle is formed by a line indicating the direction of the relative airstream. Thus, AOA is defined as the angle between the chord line of the wing and the direction of the relative wind. This is not to be confused with the angle of incidence, illustrated in Figure 1-2, which is the angle between the chord line of the wing and the longitudinal axis of the aircraft.

翼型或机翼剖面的弦是一个穿过剖面的前缘和后缘的一条假象直线。见图1-6。这个翼弦线是最终形成迎角的一部分，另一个形成部分是指示相对气流方向的直线。因此，迎角被定义为翼弦与相对气流方向的夹角。不要与安装角混淆，如图1-2所示，安装角是翼弦与飞机纵轴的夹角。

图1-6

On each part of an airfoil or wing surface, a small force is present. This force is of a different magnitude and direction from any forces acting on other areas forward or rearward from this point. It is possible to add all of these small forces mathematically. That sum is called the “resultant lift”. This resultant force has magnitude, direction, and location, and can be represented as a vector, as shown in Figure 1-7. Lift is defined as the portion of the resultant force that is perpendicular to the relative wind The point of intersection of the resultant force line with the chord line of the airfoil is called the center of pressure (CP). The CP moves along the airfoil chord as the AOA changes. Throughout most of the flight range, the CP moves forward with increasing AOA and rearward as the AOA decreases. The effect of increasing AOA on the CP is shown in Figure 1-8.

在翼型或机翼表面的每一个部分都会产生一部分很小的力， 这些力的大小和方向各不相同，通过数学方法把这些力 相加，相加的和叫做“总升力”，总升力具有，大小，方向 和位置属性，可以表示为矢量，如图1-7。升力被定义为总升力垂直于相对气流的一个分力。总升力与翼弦的 交点叫做压力中心，压力中心随迎角的变化而运动， 在大多数情况下，迎角增大，压力中心前移。增大迎角对 压力中心的影响如图1-8。

Equation of Lift
升力公式

L = C L × 1 2 ρ V 2 × S

L: Lift-升力   CL: Coefficient of Lift-升力系数   ρ: Air density-空气密度   V: velocity-速度   S: Square-机翼面积

The above lift equation exemplifies this mathematically and supports that doubling of the airspeed will result in four times the lift. As a result, one can see that velocity is an important component to the production of lift, which itself can be affected through varying AOA. When examining the equation, lift (L) is determined through the relationship of the air density (ρ), the airfoil velocity (V), the surface area of the wing (S) and the coefficient of lift (CL) for a given airfoil.

如上升力公式用数学方式证明了增大一倍空速会导致升力增大四倍的现象。 同时不难看出，速度是升力产生的一大重要部分，速度也被迎角变化影响， 在升力公式中，给定翼型的升力由大气压力，速度，机翼面积，以及升力系数之间的关系 决定。

Coefficient of Lift (CL)
升力系数

Any time the control yoke or stick is moved fore or aft, the AOA is changed. As the AOA increases, CL increases (all other factors being equal). When the aircraft reaches the maximum AOA, CL (thus Lift) begins to diminish rapidly. This is the stalling AOA, known as CL-MAX, Examine Figure 1-9, noting how the CL increases until the critical AOA is reached, then decreases rapidly with any further increase in the AOA.

在任何时刻前后移动操纵杆会使迎角发生变化。当迎角增大时， 升力系数增大（其他因素不变）。当飞机到达最大迎角时，升力系数 开始急剧减小（因此升力也急剧减小）。这就是失速迎角，对应最大升力系数， 见图1-9，注意当接近临界迎角时，升力系数先增大，然后随迎角增大急剧减小的现象。

Velocity (V)
速度

Taking the equation further, one can see an aircraft could not continue to travel in level flight at a constant altitude and maintain the same AOA if the velocity is increased. The lift would increase and the aircraft would climb as a result of the increased lift force or speed up. Therefore, to keep the aircraft straight and level (not accelerating upward) and in a state of equilibrium, as velocity is increased, lift must be kept constant. This is normally accomplished by reducing the AOA by lowering the nose. Conversely, as the aircraft is slowed, the decreasing velocity requires increasing the AOA to maintain lift sufficient to maintain flight. There is, of course, a limit to how far the AOA can be increased, if a stall is to be avoided.

根据公式更进一步，不难看出当飞机速度增大时 无法在相同的高度上以相同的迎角保持平飞。 因为升力会因速度增大而增大，使飞机上升。 因此，为使飞机保持直线平飞（不会获得向上的加速度）， 根据等式，当速度增大时，必须保持升力不变， 这通常是通过使飞机低头来减小迎角的方式实现的 。相反的，如果飞机速度减小，需要增大迎角 来保持足够的升力维持飞行。当然，如果不想失速，迎角增大要有限度。

Air density (ρ)
空气密度

Lift also vary directly with the density of the air. Density is affected by several factors: pressure, temperature, and humidity, as mentioned in earlier chapters. At an altitude of 18,000 feet, the density of the air has one-half the density of air at sea level. In order to maintain its lift at a higher altitude, an aircraft must fly at a greater true airspeed for any given AOA. Warm air is less dense than cool air, and moist air is less dense than dry air. Thus, on a hot humid day, an aircraft must be flown at a greater true airspeed for any given AOA than on a cool, dry day.

升力也会随着空气密度而变化。密度受压力，温度，和湿度的影响，这些在前面的章节中已经讲过。 在18000英尺的高度，空气密度是海平面高度上的密度的一半。为了在高高度上维持升力， 飞机在给定迎角下，必须以更大的空速飞行。温暖空气比冷空气的密度小，潮湿空气比 干燥空气的密度小。因此，在高温潮湿的天气，相比于在寒冷干燥的天气，飞机需要在给定 的迎角下以更大的真空速飞行。

If the density factor is decreased and the total lift must equal the total weight to remain in flight, it follows that one of the other factors must be increased. The factor usually increased is the airspeed or the AOA because these are controlled directly by the pilot.

如果密度减小，而升力必须与重力相等来保持飞行， 这将导致其他的影响因素增大。通常增大的因素是空速 或迎角，因为他们直接受飞行员的控制。

Wing Area (S)
机翼面积

Lift varies directly with the wing area, provided there is no change in the wing’s planform. If the wings have the same proportion and airfoil sections, a wing with a planform area of 200 square feet lifts twice as much at the same AOA as a wing with an area of 100 square feet.

升力大小直接收机翼面积的影响，假设机翼的形状不变， 机翼的比例和翼型相同，在相同迎角下，面积为200平方英尺的机翼 产生的升力为100平方英尺的机翼的两倍。

Two major aerodynamic factors from the pilot’s viewpoint are CL and airspeed because they can be controlled readily and accurately. Of course, the pilot can also control density by adjusting the altitude and can control wing area if the aircraft happens to have flaps of the type that enlarge wing area. However, for most situations, the pilot controls CL and airspeed to maneuver an aircraft. For instance, in straight-and-level flight, cruising along at a constant altitude, altitude is maintained by adjusting CL by changing AOA to match the aircraft’s velocity or cruise airspeed, while maintaining a state of equilibrium in which lift equals weight. In an approach to landing, when the pilot wishes to land as slowly as practical, it is necessary to increase AOA near maximum to maintain lift equal to the weight of the aircraft.

从飞行员的视角来看，升力系数和空速是最主要的空气动力影响因素， 因为它们可以直接并精确的受飞行员的操控。当然飞行员可以通过改变 高度的方式改变大气密度和使用有改变机翼面积功能的襟翼来控制 机翼面积。然而，在大多数情况下，飞行员控制升力系数和空速来 操控飞机。例如直线平飞，在同一高度保持巡航，飞机高度通过改变迎角来调整 升力系数于飞机的巡航速度匹配，使飞机升力与重力相等。在进近着陆过程中， 当飞行员希望着陆速度尽可能小时，需要增大迎角接近极限来保持升力与重力相等。
