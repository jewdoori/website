Wings are airfoils that, when moved rapidly through the air, create lift. They are built in many shapes and sizes. Wing design can vary to provide certain desirable flight characteristics. Control at various operating speeds, the amount of lift generated, balance, and stability all change as the shape of the wing is altered. Both the leading edge and the trailing edge of the wing may be straight or curved, or one edge may be straight and the other curved. One or both edges may be tapered so that the wing is narrower at the tip than at the root where it joins the fuselage. The wing tip may be square, rounded, or even pointed. [Figure 1-1] shows a number of typical wing leading and trailing edge shapes.

机翼由在空气中快速移动产生升力的翼型组成。不同的机翼形状大小各不相同。 机翼的设计根据所需求的特性而不同。在不同飞行速度下的操纵、产生的升力大小、平衡 和稳定性，都根据机翼的形状的变化而变化。机翼的前缘和后缘可以是都是直的 ，或是都弯曲的，也可以是一个直的，一个是弯曲的。前缘和后缘，或是其中单独一个， 都可以渐缩形成翼尖比翼根与机身连接处窄的机翼形状。 翼尖可以做成矩形、弧形甚至是尖头的。[图1-1] 列出了几个典型机翼的前缘和后缘的形状。

The wings of an aircraft can be attached to the fuselage at the top, mid-fuselage, or at the bottom. These designs are referred to as high-, mid-, and low-wing, respectively. The number of wings can also vary. Airplanes with a single set of wings are referred to as monoplanes, while those with two sets are called biplanes. They may extend perpendicular to the horizontal plane of the fuselage or can angle up or down slightly. This angle is known as the wing dihedral. The dihedral angle affects the lateral stability of the aircraft. [Figure 1-2] shows some common wing attach points and dihedral angle. [Figure 1-2] shows specific types of monoplane and biplane.

机翼可安装在机身的上部，中部或是底部。这些机翼位置设计分别被称作上、中、下单翼。机翼对数也有不同设计，只有一对机翼的飞机被称作单翼机，有两对的被称作双翼机。机翼水平方向垂直穿过机身， 或略微向上或向下倾斜一个角度。这个角度叫机翼上反角。上反角影响 飞机的横侧稳定性。[图 1-2]列出了几个常见的机翼安装位置和上反角。[图1-3]列出了特定型号的单翼机和双翼机。

Often wings are of full cantilever design. This means they are built so that no external bracing is needed. They are supported internally by structural members assisted by the skin of the aircraft. Other aircraft wings use external struts or wires to assist in supporting the wing and carrying the aerodynamic and landing loads. Wing support cables and struts are generally made from steel. Many struts and their attach fittings have fairings to reduce drag. Short, nearly vertical supports called jury struts are found on struts that attach to the wings a great distance from the fuselage. This serves to subdue strut movement and oscillation caused by the air flowing around the strut in flight. [Figure 1-5] shows samples of wings using external bracing, also known as semicantilever wings. Cantilever wings built with no external bracing are also shown.

大部分机翼采用悬臂式设计，这意味着这些机翼不需要外部支撑。它们 由内部结构支撑，并由飞机蒙皮辅助支撑。其他的飞机机翼采用外部撑杆 或钢索辅助支撑机翼，并承受空气动力载荷和着陆载荷。支撑钢索和撑杆 一般由钢铁制成。很多撑杆和它们的接头安装有整流罩来减小阻力。短小， 并近乎垂直的支撑杆叫辅助撑杆，辅助撑杆安装在撑杆离机身很远的位置上并链接机翼。 这是为了减小撑杆在飞行中由空气流动的位移和振动。[图1-5]例举了使用外部 支撑的飞机，又叫半悬臂式机翼，以及没有外部支撑的悬臂式机翼。
