Airfoil
翼型

An airfoil is a surface designed to obtain lift from the air through which it moves. Thus, it can be stated that any part of the aircraft that converts air resistance into lift is an airfoil. The profile of a conventional wing is an excellent example of an airfoil.

翼型是设计用于在空气中运动来获得升力的物面。因此，翼型可被表述为飞机的任意一处可以将空气阻力转换成升力的部位。 传统机翼的剖面就是翼型的典型例子。

Upper/ lower surface (camber)
上/下表面（弧线）

The upper and lower surfaces are sometimes referred to as upper and lower camber. [Figure 1-1]
上表面和下表面有时被称作上弧线和下弧线。[图1-1]

Leading edge & Trailing edge
前缘 & 后缘

The two extremities of the airfoil profile: The front of the wing profile, which faces forward in flight, is called the leading edge; the back of the wing profile is called the trailing edge. [Figure 1-2]

翼剖面的两端：翼剖面在飞行中朝前的一端叫做前缘；后端叫做后缘。[图1-2]

Chord line
翼弦

A reference line often used in discussing the airfoil is the chord line, a straight line drawn through the profile connecting the extremities of the leading and trailing edges. [Figure 1-3]

在翼型的相关内容中经常使用的基准线是翼弦，翼弦是穿过剖面，连接前缘和后缘的直线。[图1-3]

Mean camber line & Camber
中弧线 & 弯度

Mean camber line
中弧线

Another reference line, drawn from the leading edge to the trailing edge, is the mean camber line. This mean line is equidistant at all points from the upper and lower surfaces. [Figure 1-4]

从前缘画到后缘的另一条基准线叫做中弧线，这条线在任意点距离上下表面的距离相等。[图1-4]

For a symmetrical airfoil, it is merged with the chord line. [Figure 1-5]
对于对称翼型，中弧线与翼弦重合。[图1-5]

Camber
弯度

The maximum distance from the chord line to the camber line is designated as the airfoil camber. [Figure 1-6] Camber generally expressed as a percent of the chord length, such as 5% camber.

从翼弦到中弧线的最大距离称作翼型的弯度。[图1-6] 弯度一般用相对于翼弦长度的百分比表示，例如5%弯度。

Thickness & Fineness ratio
厚度 & 长细比

Thickness
厚度

The maximum distance between the upper and lower surface is the airfoil thickness, also designate as a percent of chord length. [Fiugre 1-6]

翼型上下表面之间的最大距离称作翼型厚度，同样用翼弦长度的百分比表示。[图1-6]

Fineness ratio
长细比

Fineness ratio is defined as the ratio of the chord of the airfoil to the maximum thickness. If the wing has a high fineness ratio, it is a very thin wing. A thick wing has a low fineness ratio.

长细比的定义是翼型的翼弦与最大厚度的比例。 大长细比的机翼很薄，很厚的机翼长细比很低。

Leading Edge Radius
前缘半径

Leading edge radius is the radius of the circle centered at a point on the mean camber line, and tangent to the upper and lower surfaces at the forward-most edge of an airfoil. [Figure 1-7]
